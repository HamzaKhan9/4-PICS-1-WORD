          4 Pics 1 Word is a word guessing game. It presents you with four
          pictures and then tasks you with guessing what specific word fits with
          the theme of the photos presented.<br />
          Their a scoring system in it in which you can compete with your
          friends.<br />
          Their are three stages in the game (EASY, MEDIUM AND HARD).<br />
          Level 1 -20 is the easy stage of the game.<br />
          Level 21-40 is the medium stage of the game.<br />
          And Level 21-50 is the hard stage of the game.<br /><br />
          <span>HOW SCORING WORKS:-</span><br />
          Their is a timer on top of the game starting from 0 seconds.<br />
          &rarr; In easy stage if you guess the word in less than 30s you will
          get 8 points and if you guess the word in less than 60s you will get 7
          points. More than 60s will only reward you 5 points.<br />
          &rarr; In medium stage if you guess the word in less than 30s you will
          get 13 points and if you guess the word in less than 60s you will get
          12 points. More than 60s will only reward you 10 points.<br />
          &rarr; In hard stage if you guess the word in less than 30s you will
          get 18 points and if you guess the word in less than 60s you will get
          17 points. More than 60s will only reward you 15 points.<br />
